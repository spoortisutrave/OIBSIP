Satya Nadella

CEO of Microsoft Corporation

Satya Nadella is an Indian-American business executive who is the current CEO of Microsoft Corporation. He was born on August 19, 1967, in Hyderabad, India. Nadella completed his Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Manipal Institute of Technology and went on to pursue a Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Wisconsin.

Nadella joined Microsoft in 1992 and has held several key positions in the company, including Executive Vice President of the Cloud and Enterprise Group, and President of the Server and Tools Division. In 2014, he was appointed as the CEO of Microsoft, succeeding Steve Ballmer.

Under Nadella's leadership, Microsoft has focused on cloud computing and AI, and has launched several new products and services, including Microsoft Teams, HoloLens, and Microsoft Surface. He has also been a vocal advocate for diversity and inclusion in the technology industry.

Outside of Microsoft, Nadella serves on the Board of Directors of Starbucks and is a member of the Board of Trustees of the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center. He is also the author of the book "Hit Refresh: The Quest to Rediscover Microsoft's Soul and Imagine a Better Future for Everyone".
